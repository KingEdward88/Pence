The Rajek!

Abs of steel she looks unreal

Her heavenly figure’s my new meal

And when she walks her line gets taught

By heavens gate I’ll wait with luck

For one swift kick I’ll move in quick

To devour her, my only pick…

    - Chorus -

To see her shine and call her mine,

To see her shine her hand in mine,

To see her shine with hearts entwined,

To see her shine without me.

When heaven opens up her door

You’ll walk home with hearts adore

And when you speak they’ll beg for more

The Rajek will surprise me!

It appears as though the young Veronika has blocked me on X.com;  I am very sad.  This may be my last letter to the Vivacious Veronika, but I guess all good things must come to an end.  I wish you the very best Veronika and I know good things will find you.  

With much admiration and a touch of love,

His Majesty King James Douglas Edward of Britain
